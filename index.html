Nippon Koei India Pvt. Ltd.

Nippon Koei India Pvt. Ltd. (NK India) is an ISO 9001:2015, 14001:2015 & 45001:2018 certified company and is a wholly owned subsidiary of Integrated Design & Engineering Holdings Co., Ltd. of Japan (ID&E). NK India, headquartered in New Delhi, focuses on projects in India and other developing countries. Formed in 2008, it is managed and staffed with highly experienced Professionals with significant overseas consulting experience. NK India has more than 800 employees and most of our resource personnel are with post-graduation, technically qualified, trained and self-driven in completing our projects across India.
